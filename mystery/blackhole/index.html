BLACK HOLES

THE DEVOURERS OF LIGHT AND TIME

🌀 WHAT IS A BLACK HOLE?

Universe-ile ettavum powerful and mysterious aaya monsters aanu Black Holes. Ithoru vacuum cleaner pole aanu—gravity atra strong aaya kond light-inu polum ithil ninnu escape cheyyan kazhiyilla. Oru giant star marikkumpol (Supernova) athu swantham gravity kaaranam crush cheyyappedumbol aanu oru black hole janikkunnath.

🌌 THE ANATOMY OF A MONSTER

Oru Black hole-il mukkya bhagangal ivayaanu:

PART WHAT IT IS
Event Horizon The "Point of No Return". Ithu cross cheythal pinne thirichu varaan kazhiyilla.
Singularity Black hole-inte center. Ivide physics-ile ella rules-um fail aakum.
Accretion Disk Black hole-inu chuttum parakkunna glowing gas and dust.

⏳ TIME TRAVEL & DILATION

Einstein-te Theory of Relativity prakaram, gravity kooduthal ulla sthalath **Time slow aakum**.

👉 The Interstellar Effect: Oru black hole-inu aduthu poyi ningal 1 hour spend cheythaal, Earth-il 7 varshangal kazhinjirunnekkam! Black hole sathyathil oru Natural Time Machine aanu.

🍝 SPAGHETTIFICATION (DREADFUL DEATH)

Ningal oru black hole-ilekk veezhukayaanenkil enthu sambhavikkum? Ningalude kaal-ile gravity, thalaye-kkaal kooduthal aayirikkum. Ithu kaaranam ningalude body oru Noodle pole stretch cheyyappedum. Ithine science-il Spaghettification ennu vilikkunnu.

🛸 THE WORMHOLE THEORY

Chila scientists parayunnath Black hole-inte ullil pokunnath matoru universe-ilekkulla shortcut aavaam ennanu.

  • White Holes: Black hole everything ullilekk edukkumpol, White Hole everything purathekku thallunnu.
  • Multiverse: Namukk ariyatha matoru dimension-ilekkulla 'Portal' aavaam ithu.
"Black holes are where God divided by zero."

🧩 FINAL THOUGHT

Black holes namme ormippikkunnath nammude arivu ethra cheriya aanu ennaanu. Lokathey ettavum veliya rahasyam ippozhum aa 'Dark Void'-inu ullil maranju kidakkunnu. Maybe one day, we will look into the abyss and it will reveal the **Origin of Everything.** 😌
