🌐 DARK WEB

DEEP SECRETS & DIGITAL BORDERS

🧠 THE LAYERS OF INTERNET

Nammal use cheyyunna internet oru Iceberg pole aanu. Mukalil kaanunnath kurachu mathram!

• Surface Web (4%): Google, YouTube, Facebook. Ellavarkkum access cheyyam.
• Deep Web (90%): Banking data, Emails, Government records. Private aanu pakshe illegal alla.
• Dark Web (6%): Hidden networks. Special software (TOR) illathe access cheyyan kazhiyilla.

💻 HOW IT WORKS? (TOR EXPLAINED)

Dark web access cheyyan TOR (The Onion Router) browser venam. Ithu normal browsers pole alla. Nammude connection pala layers of encryption vazhi pass cheyyunnu (athukondaanu 'Onion' ennu vilikkunnath).

👉 Anonymity: Ithil IP address hide cheyyappedum. Aaru, evide ninnu connect cheyyunnu ennu kandupidikkan valare prayasam aanu.

🏛️ THE LEGEND OF SILK ROAD

Dark web-ine famous aakkiyath Silk Road enna marketplace aanu. Ross Ulbricht (Dread Pirate Roberts) 2011-il ithu start cheythu. Ithu oru "Amazon for illegal items" aayirunnu, evide Bitcoin upayogichu enthu venamenkilum vangi-vikkamayirunnu. Pakshe 2013-il FBI ithu shut down cheythu.

🌌 MARIANA'S WEB (THE ULTIMATE MYTH)

Internet-il ulla ettavum valiya urban legend aanu Mariana's Web. Internet-inte deep-most layer aayi ithine parayunnu.

⚠️ The Legend: Parayappedunnath, ithu access cheyyan Quantum Computers venam ennanu. "Closed Shell Systems" vazhi ulla ithil world secrets, AI controls okke undennu parayunnu. (Note: Ithu kooduthalum oru Creepypasta story mathram aanu!)

🎥 REALITY VS YOUTUBE STORIES

Chila creators parayunna "Red Rooms" (Live crimes) sathyathil dark web-inte bandwidth-il stream cheyyan valare prayasam aanu. Most of them are Scams to steal your Bitcoins.

👉 RED ROOMS: 99% of them are FAKE. Just money stealing scams aanu.

⚠️ DANGER: NOT JUST HACKERS

Ningal verum curiosity kond dark web-il keriyal undakaavunna real risks:

• Phishing: Ningalude real identity hack cheyyappedam.
• Illegal Content: Ariyathe polum illegal content view cheyyunnath legal issues undakkum.
• Exit Nodes: Ningal TOR use cheyyumpol computer-ile traffic monitor cheyyan hackers-inu kazhiyum.

NEVER use your real name, email, or photo anywhere on the dark web 🛑

⚖️ THE POSITIVE SIDE (SATHYAM ENTHANU?)

Dark web verum "criminal world" mathram alla:

✔️ Journalists: Dictatorship ulla countries-il ninnu news purathu vidan use cheyyunnu.
✔️ Whistleblowers: Government corruption purathu vidan (Ex: Edward Snowden) use cheyyunnu.
✔️ Privacy: Tracking bhayamillathe communicate cheyyan uthamam aanu.

🧩 FINAL THOUGHT

Dark web oru Hammer (chuttika) pole aanu. Athu vechu veedu paniyam, thala adichu pottikkukayum cheyyam. Athu use cheyyunnavante manassu pole irikkum.
